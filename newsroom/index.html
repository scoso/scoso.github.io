Open enrollment will continue for two more weeks. Now is the time to make changes to your plan or enroll in a plan as a new member. Free Flu Shot for Harvard Pilgrim Members

Harvard Pilgrim members, 19 years and older, can get flu vaccines from participating flu vaccine pharmacies (e.g. CVS, Walgreens, Rite Aid). Just present your ID card and the pharmacy will process the flu vaccine with no co-pay.

Published January 15, 2016

Tags: flu shot, plan benefits

(Hartford, CT) Harvard Pilgrim Health Care announced today that its Connecticut membership has grown to more than 24,000, exceeding expectations for 2015. It now serves more than 800 Connecticut businesses.

Harvard Pilgrim Health Care Earns Top Marks in 2016 Corporate Equality Index

(Wellesley, MA) Harvard Pilgrim Health Care has received a perfect score on the 2016 Corporate Equality Index (CEI), a national benchmarking survey and report on corporate policies and practices related to LGBT workplace equality, administered by the Human Rights Campaign Foundation.
