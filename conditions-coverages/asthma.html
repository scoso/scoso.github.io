Sample costs & savings

Meet Sample Susan

We can't tell you what your annual cost would be, but we can show you an example. The following table shows how Sample Susan (an asthma sufferer in her 30s) might use her plan in a year.

This is a table showing sample costs that a Harvard Pilgrim member might pay for asthma medications and appointments.
Service Susan Pays Frequency Annual cost
Primary care visit $25 3 per year $75
Advair (tier 2 drug)* $25 40 refills $1,000
Pulmonologist $100 Once per year $100
Allergist $50 Once per year $50

Susan's total annual cost: $1,225

*Susan could save if she switches to a generic Tier 1 drug like Genericol. She'd save $600 a year (Tier 1 refills are only $10).

Compared to uninsured:

Susan would pay $6,000 for these services without health insurance.
