How to get medication

Retail

Use MedImpact Healthcare System's Pharmacy Locater to find a retail pharmacy near you.

Pharmacy locater (offsite)

Mail Order

Harvard Pilgrim's Mail Service Program is for maintenance medications and health products. You'll get free shipping on deliveries of a 90-day supply of your medications to your home. Order new prescriptions and refills online, or print out the Mail Service Order Form and mail it to the Walgreens address on the form, or call 1-877-347-3216 and tell them your ID number, prescription number(s) and credit card information to pay for the prescriptions.

Online prescriptions & refills (offsite) Mail service order form

Specialty

For specialty medications, call the Accredo Health Group, Inc. at (866) 815-4717, or Magellan Health at (866) 554-2673. They'll provide you with emergency overnight or same day delivery via mail or courier, around-the-clock telephone access to nurses and clinical pharmacists, and educational materials about your medication. See if your medication is considered a specialty medication.

Check eligibility

Infertility

If you're taking medication for infertility, order it from the Freedom Drug at (877) 585-4603, or Village Fertility Pharmacy at (877) 334-1610, or at IVPCARE, a Walgreens Specialty Company at (800) 424-9002. They'll provide you with emergency overnight or same day delivery via mail or courier, around-the-clock telephone access to nurses and clinical pharmacists, and educational materials about your medication. See if your medication is considered an infertility medication.

Check eligibility

If your medication is not covered by Harvard Pilgrim because it is excluded or limited, you may request an exception by calling the Pharmacy Services Department, at (888) 888–4742 ext. 31786. For a faster decision, please include the medical information from your prescribing provider when you first ask for an exception. Your prescribing provider may also send a Formulary Exception Request Form or a statement of Medical Necessity by fax at (617) 509–9144 or email to pharmacy_services@harvardpilgrim.org.
