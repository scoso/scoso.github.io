Reimbursement requirements*

If enrolled through an employer, the employer must offer Harvard Pilgrim's fitness reimbursement program. If enrolled through a Harvard Pilgrim Buy Direct plan, you'll be eligible after four months in the plan.

Health club membership must be for at least four months in a current calendar year.

For most plans, the subscriber must be active with coverage that includes the Fitness Reimbursement program at the time Harvard Pilgrim receives a completed Fitness Reimbursement Form. Please see your Benefit Handbook and Schedule of Benefits for details on your specific plan.

Current Harvard Pilgrim membership must be at least four months with the same employer group (or enrolled in a Buy Direct plan for at least four months) in a calendar year and must coincide with four months of gym membership.

You can submit for reimbursement starting with May 1 of the current calendar year* and when you have met the above-stated criteria.

Qualified, full-service health/fitness clubs have cardiovascular and strength-training equipment and facilities for exercising and improving physical fitness. Validation as full-service is subject to approval by Harvard Pilgrim.

Facilities/programs that DO NOT qualify for reimbursement include: Martial arts centers, gymnastics facilities, classes, country clubs, fees for personal trainers, tennis, aerobic or pool-only facilities, as well as sports teams and leagues.

* If you are enrolled through an employer that offers this program upon the annual enrollment/anniversary date, you will be eligible four months after the enrollment/anniversary date. For example: If your employer's enrollment/anniversary date is July 1, eligibility to submit for reimbursement begins no earlier than November 1 (as long as all other criteria is met, including being an active member).

Plan benefits for vision

Your Harvard Pilgrim PPO plan covers one eye exam every two years.

In addition, you get lots of discounts/savings on Frames, Glasses lenses, Contacts, and Lasik just for being a Harvard Pilgrim member. To get these deals, show your Harvard Pilgrim member ID card when making your purchase.

Your plan includes coverage for some prescription eyewear. These stores offer additional discounts.

  • LensCrafters – 35% off frames
  • Sears Optical– 35% off frames
  • Target Optical– 35% off frames
  • Pearle Vision– 35% off frames
  • JC Penney Optical– 35% off frames
  • Other participating eyewear providers– 35% off frames
  • Visionworks – 40% off frames (Some collections excluded)
  • Harvard Pilgrim Platinum Collection: $49.95 for frames (with single vision, non-coated plastic lenses)
  • Harvard Pilgrim Designer Collection: $99.95 for frames (with single vision, non-coated plastic lenses)
  • With a covered exam, you get FREE frames from the Harvard Pilgrim Silver Collection
  • Harvard Vanguard Medical Associates – 40% off frames
